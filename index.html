About Me

Hi, I’m Ohik, a Computer Science student at the University of Illinois-Chicago. I picked up coding as a hobby in 2020 as part of a challenge to learn something new during the pandemic. Throughout the years, I’ve taken the initiative to enroll in courses and join organizations to enhance my skills. Now, what started as simple Tic-Tac-Toe games built on Scratch have evolved into more complex projects that combine a multitude of languages and skills. I’m extremely proud of my journey so far, and I can’t wait to see what new opportunities will teach me. When I’m away from my computer, I love to play Volleyball, listen to music, keep up-to-date on emerging technologies, and spend quality time with my friends and family.
